# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview
SleepBetter is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal health data.

## Data Collection
- **Local Storage Only**: All sleep data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not upload your sleep data to any server
- **No Personal Information**: We do not require registration or login
- **No Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services

## HealthKit
If you choose to connect SleepBetter to Apple Health:
- Data shared is limited to sleep records you explicitly export
- HealthKit data remains under your control
- You can disconnect at any time in Settings

## Subscriptions
- Payment processing handled by Apple App Store
- Subscription management through iOS Settings
- We do not receive your payment information

## Children's Privacy
SleepBetter is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy
If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted in the app.

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact us through the App Store support page.
