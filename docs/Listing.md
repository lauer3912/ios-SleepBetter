## Description

**Your Journey to Better Sleep Starts Here**

SleepBetter is the ultimate sleep companion designed to help you achieve restful, rejuvenating sleep every night. Track your sleep patterns, discover personalized insights, and wake up feeling refreshed.

### Key Features

**Sleep Tracking**
- Automatically track sleep duration and quality
- Deep sleep, light sleep, and REM analysis
- Sleep efficiency scoring
- Apple Health integration

**Smart Alarm**
- Wake up gently during your lightest sleep phase
- Customizable wake-up window (15-45 minutes)
- Gentle wake sounds that gradually increase

**Sleep Sounds**
- 20+ premium sleep sounds: white noise, nature, ambient
- Mix multiple sounds for your perfect environment
- Sleep timer with gradual fade-out

**Sleep Insights**
- Weekly and monthly sleep reports
- Personalized recommendations
- Track sleep trends over time

**Bedtime Reminders**
- Customizable bedtime notifications
- Build healthy sleep habits
- Track bedtime consistency

### Premium Features ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)
- Advanced sleep analytics
- Unlimited sleep history
- Export sleep data
- Priority support

### Privacy
Your sleep data is stored securely on your device and never shared. Premium features require subscription.
