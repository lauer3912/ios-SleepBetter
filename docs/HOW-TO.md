# SleepBetter - How to Use This App

## Getting Started

### First Launch
1. Open SleepBetter on your device
2. Grant notification permissions for bedtime reminders
3. Set your target sleep hours (default: 8 hours)
4. Configure your wake-up time

### Navigation
- **Home**: View last night's sleep summary and quick actions
- **History**: Browse your sleep records and trends
- **Sounds**: Play relaxing sounds to help you fall asleep
- **Settings**: Customize app preferences

## Tracking Sleep

### Manual Sleep Tracking
1. Tap "Sleep Now" on the Home screen when you go to bed
2. When you wake up, tap the sun icon
3. Your sleep will be recorded with quality analysis

### Sleep Quality Factors
- Duration of deep sleep
- Number of times you woke up
- Consistency of your sleep schedule
- Time spent in REM sleep

## Using Sleep Sounds

### Playing Sounds
1. Go to the Sounds tab
2. Select a category (White Noise, Nature, Ambient, Meditation)
3. Tap any sound to start playback
4. Use the mini-player to pause or stop

### Sound Categories
- **White Noise**: Pure white noise, fan, pink noise
- **Nature**: Rain, ocean waves, forest, birds
- **Ambient**: Bedroom, fireplace, café
- **Meditation**: Deep breathing, guided sleep

## Smart Alarm

### Setting an Alarm
1. Go to Settings → Alarms
2. Enable "Smart Alarm"
3. Set your desired wake-up time
4. The app will wake you during your lightest sleep phase

### Gentle Wake
When enabled, the alarm will start quietly and gradually increase volume over 30 seconds.

## Sleep Insights

### Weekly Report
Every Monday, you'll receive a summary of your sleep from the past week, including:
- Average sleep duration
- Sleep quality trends
- Best and worst nights
- Personalized tips

### Data Export
Export your sleep history as CSV:
1. Go to History tab
2. Tap the share icon
3. Choose "Export Data"
4. Select CSV format

## Tips for Better Sleep

1. **Consistent Schedule**: Go to bed and wake up at the same time daily
2. **Wind Down**: Use sleep sounds 30 minutes before bed
3. **Avoid Screens**: Turn off devices 1 hour before sleep
4. **Optimize Environment**: Keep your bedroom cool (65-68°F)
5. **Track Regularly**: The more you track, the better the insights

## Troubleshooting

**Sleep not tracking correctly**
- Ensure the app has notification permissions
- Check that your device isn't in Do Not Disturb mode

**Sounds won't play**
- Check that your device isn't muted
- Ensure Bluetooth isn't connected to another audio device

**Alarm didn't go off**
- Verify notification permissions
- Check that Do Not Disturb is disabled

## Privacy

All sleep data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share any personal health information. Premium features require subscription but your data remains private.
